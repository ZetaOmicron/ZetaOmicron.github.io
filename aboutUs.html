Zeta Omciron is a nationally recognized Computer Science Honor Society, founded in 2013 by a group of students from Klein Oak High School. The honor society has expanded to two chapters including the original in Houston, Texas and a sister branch in Berkeley, California.
Zeta Omicron members partake in many activities such as competitive programming contests, tutoring, and fundraising, with strong emphasis on Collaborative Learning and Service.
