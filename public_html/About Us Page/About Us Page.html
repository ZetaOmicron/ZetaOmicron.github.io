About Us
Zeta Omicron was founded by a group of students at Klein Oak High School in 2013. As of 2015, Zeta Omicron has two chapters in both Houston, Texas and Berkeley, California.
Zeta Omicron focuses on two aspects during it's activities: Collaborative Learning and Service. We partake in many activities such as competitive programming contests, tutoring, and fundraising.
